Our Story: The Taguiporo Experience

Discover the heart of Ilocano tranquility

Nestled along the serene shores of Paoay Lake in Ilocos Norte, Taguiporo Resort was founded with a single vision: to offer a tranquil escape that blends natural Ilocano beauty with modern luxury.

We pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality, fresh local cuisine, and breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding sand dunes. Every corner of the resort is designed to help you relax and reconnect with nature.

Our History

The history of "Taguipuro Paoay Lake" is not about a separate location, but refers to the Paoay Lake itself and the area along its shores. The lake is a freshwater body that formed approximately 6,000 years ago when a sandbar spit separated it from the sea, and the sandbar is now home to the Paoay Sand Dunes. Legends suggest the area was once a prosperous town that was flooded as a punishment, while the lake is now a national park and a popular spot for recreation and a rich habitat for various bird species.
